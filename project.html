Yarn Index

Deciding the yarn weight and fiber of the yarn you are going to work on for your projects is the first step of any project. If you're yarn weight is too large then your project will come out too big, but if you choose a yarn weight that is too small then your project will have a lot of space in between your stitches creating loose stitches. The yarn weight refers to the amount of strands are twisted together in order to make the yarn. For instance, a 1-Fingering yarn has one strand of yarn, whereas, a 4-worsted has four strands twisted together. The fiber content is just as important because different fibers are ideal for different projects. Natural fibers are good for projects such as garments, and artificial fibers are good for projects such as blankets or amigurumi. This Yarn Index will help you to see some different types of yarns in different sizes by the two most popular yarn sellers, there are many more types available.
